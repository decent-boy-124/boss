GTTI Rahim Yar Khan is a government technical training institute under TEVTA Punjab, offering diplomas and certificates in technical and vocational fields.

The institute offers diplomas and certificates in IT, Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, Electronics, Auto, Welding, HVAC, and other technical trades.

Generally, candidates who have completed Matric (10th grade) are eligible. Some short courses may require Middle (8th grade) only.

Admission forms are available at the institute office. Students can also apply through the TEVTA Punjab official website when admissions are open.

Diplomas usually range from 1 to 2 years, while certificates and short courses vary between 3 to 6 months.

About Us

Government Technical Training Institute (GTTI) Rahim Yar Khan is one of the leading vocational and technical institutes under TEVTA Punjab. The institute is dedicated to providing quality technical education, modern skills, and hands-on training to meet the needs of local industries as well as international job markets. With a wide range of diploma and certificate programs, GTTI focuses on empowering youth with practical knowledge in engineering, information technology, mechanical, and vocational trades. The institute is equipped with skilled instructors, updated laboratories, and industry-relevant curricula. Over the years, GTTI Rahim Yar Khan has produced thousands of skilled graduates who are successfully serving in Pakistan and abroad. Its mission is to bridge the gap between education and employment by preparing students for modern careers and entrepreneurship..
